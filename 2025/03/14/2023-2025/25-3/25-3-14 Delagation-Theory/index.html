Delagation Theory

Created2025-03-14|Updated2025-04-18|2023-202525-3
|Post Views:

Baird, Aaron, and Likoebe M. Maruping. “The Next Generation of Research on IS Use: A Theoretical Framework of Delegation to and from Agentic IS Artifacts.” MIS Quarterly 45, no. 1 (March 1, 2021): 315–41. https://doi.org/10.25300/MISQ/2021/15882.

Presumption: the theoretical paradigm shift in the ==IS use== research - from “treating the IS artifact as a passive tool” to “agentic IS artifacts”.

Gap: The vast majority of IS use research, however, assumes that IS artifacts are tools that serve as a means to achieving a user’s ends.

Core:

  1. transferring rights and responsibilities for task execution and outcomes. [1]
  2. This new generation of agentic IS artifacts is imbued with the capacity to learn, adapt, act autonomously, and be aware of the need to act without being prompted by users. [2]

[^1]: 可以理解为“自组织”，“自进化”的观点。比如设计一套算法，在给予初始化的数据后，获得了一个结果。这个是传统的IS use所关注的，但是现在每天都有大量的数据投喂进这个算法，使得产出的结果出现了变化，而这里面，human responsibilities减少了，单纯的是Agentic IS artifacts在运作
[^2]: 许多基于人的责任在转移给新的agent，例如，汽车驾驶——>智能驾驶，这里面的agent从司机变成了program
